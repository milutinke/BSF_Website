Balkans Special Forces Gaming

General information about the community

Who are we?

Balkans Special Forces (BSF) is a gaming community dedicated to gaming in an SFW environment, making new friendships, and relaxed play.

We all have our own lives and real-life responsibilities, so, we are a relaxed type of a community, you play when you are in the mood and when you have time, no one is going to force you to keep with some sort of schedule.

What games do we play?

Currently, we are centered around the game called Squad from Offworld Industries. But, we also play Escape From Tarkov and ARMA III.

We are looking to expand to other games once we have gathered a sufficient number of members and completed the formation of the International Clan.

Is this community Balkans exclusive?

No, do not let the name fool you, we may have a lot of people from the Balkans, but we are international. We have started on the Balkans, but we have a lot of international members from all over the world.

Do we have any servers?

Yes, we do, we currently host 2 Squad servers, one is a public vanilla server and the other one is for threening/events.

Information related to Clan(s) and the Battalion

Clans?

Yes, it is plural, Clans, we currently have 2 clans. The original one, Balkans exclusive clan (by Balkans we mean Ex. Yugoslav states) and the International clan.

Balkans exclusive clan

The members of this clan are only people from ex. Yugoslav states, but if you know our language on a sufficient level, you can join in. This is the original clan that has been formed last year, currently, we count over 160 people, but we are growing day by day. The clan tag is BSF.

International Clan

Despite us being from the Balkans, we also have an international version of the clan with the tag BSF-I. By default, the members of the original BSF clan are also members of the International (BSF-I) clan, but, without them, currently, this clan counts about 20++ members. We are looking to expand and get more international members for this clan.

Batallion

Currently, the original BSF clan has formed up a Battalion that will represent the community/clan on the competitive scene. The Battalion is currently in process of training members.
